An Inspired School

Embracing individuality, nurturing talent, and preparing young men to lead in a global community.

Globally Connected, Locally Rooted

Paul Boit Boys High School is proud to be a founding member of the Pan-African Excellence Network, a collaborative group of leading secondary schools across the continent. Through this network, our students connect with peers in Uganda, Tanzania, Nigeria, Ghana, and South Africa via joint academic projects, sports exchanges, and cultural programmes.

Our teachers and leadership team benefit from shared best practices, professional development workshops, and an international perspective that enriches classroom learning. We aim to nurture every individual with robust teaching methods and a personalised approach, preparing them for achievement in Kenya and the world beyond.

The Three Pillars of Our Educational Approach

As a school rooted in Kenyan values yet globally connected, we focus on three core pillars that underpin our holistic methodology, equipping every boy with the skills to thrive.

Academic Excellence

A rigorous CBC curriculum delivered by passionate teachers, with a focus on STEM, research, and critical thinking. Our students consistently rank among the top in the region.

Global Perspective

Through exchange programmes, virtual collaborations, and international guest speakers, our boys develop a worldview that prepares them for tertiary education anywhere.

Character & Leadership

We build resilience, integrity, and empathy through community service, student leadership, and mentorship. Our graduates are not just scholars – they are gentlemen.
