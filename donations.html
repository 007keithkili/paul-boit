Together We Build

Paul Boit Boys High School is a public institution, but government funding alone cannot cover all our needs. Donations from alumni, parents, and friends enable us to provide exceptional facilities, scholarships, and programmes that transform lives.

Your support – whether large or small – helps us maintain our tradition of excellence and ensures that every boy, regardless of background, can access quality education in a nurturing environment.

Your Impact in Action

Thanks to past donations, we have:

  • Built a modern science laboratory – now serving 300+ students weekly.
  • Provided 50 full scholarships to bright students from disadvantaged backgrounds.
  • Renovated the school library with over 2,000 new books and e‑learning stations.
  • Upgraded sports facilities – new rugby posts, athletics track, and equipment.

"I donated to build the new computer lab because I believe in giving back. Seeing the boys' excitement when they use the facility is priceless."

— Eng. Paul Kipchumba (Alumnus, Class of 1998)
