Life at Paul Boit Boys

More than academics – a vibrant community of sports, arts, leadership, and brotherhood

A Brotherhood Forged Daily

At Paul Boit Boys, every day is an opportunity to grow – in the classroom, on the field, and in character. Our students live, learn, and thrive together in a structured yet warm boarding environment.

From morning preps to evening house gatherings, from sports tournaments to music festivals, we ensure each boy discovers and nurtures his unique talents. Here, lifelong friendships are formed and leaders are made.

A Day in the Life

Morning

6:00 am wake-up, physical training, then breakfast. House meetings set the tone for the day.

Academics

Engaging lessons from 8:00 am to 3:30 pm, with practicals in labs and ICT suites.

Afternoon

Sports, clubs, or personal time. Co-curricular activities run daily from 4:00 pm.

Evening

Preps, house dinners, and mentorship sessions. Lights out at 10:00 pm.

Champions on the Field

Our sports programme is a source of pride. With top-tier coaching and facilities, we compete and win at regional and national levels. Rugby, soccer, volleyball, athletics – every boy finds his sport.

We believe sports build discipline, teamwork, and resilience. Regular inter-house and inter-school fixtures keep the competitive spirit alive.

  • Regional rugby champions 2024
  • Soccer semi-finalists nationals
  • Athletics individual gold medals

Creative Expressions

From the stage to the canvas, our boys explore their artistic sides. The annual drama festival, music concerts, and art exhibitions are highlights of the school calendar.

Students can join the choir, GLEE club, or learn an instrument. Our journalism club produces a school magazine, and the photography club captures every moment.

We encourage every boy to participate – whether performing, painting, or behind the scenes.

Moments That Matter

"Paul Boit Boys isn't just a school; it's a family. The camaraderie, the support from teachers, and the opportunities to lead have shaped me into the man I am today."

— Brian Kiprop, Head Prefect 2024

Leadership & Service

We believe in nurturing future leaders. Through the prefectship system, peer mentoring, and community service projects, our boys learn responsibility and empathy.

From organising charity drives to leading clubs, every student has a chance to make a difference. Our leadership programmes prepare them for university and beyond.
