Dr. Kevin Murgor

Class of 2005

Chief Economist at the Central Bank of Kenya. Dr. Murgor has been instrumental in shaping national economic policy. He regularly mentors current students and sponsors the annual economics club conference.

"Paul Boit Boys taught me discipline and the value of hard work. The foundation I received here was critical to my success."
