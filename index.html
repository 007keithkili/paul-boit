Welcome to Paul Boit Boys

Established in 1985, Paul Boit Boys High School is a public boys boarding senior school in Turbo Sub-county, Uasin Gishu County. We offer a holistic CBC education across three pathways, nurturing young men of character and academic excellence.

Our Guiding Principles

Our Mission

To nurture disciplined, responsible, and academically excellent young men through quality education, mentorship, and character development.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic and co-curricular excellence producing holistic leaders who positively impact society.

Mean Target

We aim for a mean score of 10.5 in KCSE by 2027, with 100% transition to tertiary institutions.

Why Choose Paul Boit?

Academic Excellence

Consistent top KCSE results with experienced teachers and modern resources.

Holistic Development

Strong sports, music, drama, and clubs – rugby champions, science congress qualifiers.

Character Building

Values: Integrity, Resilience, Excellence, Teamwork. We shape responsible gentlemen.

Our Campus

Located in Turbo Sub-county, Uasin Gishu County, our expansive campus features modern classrooms, science labs, a library, and sports fields – all with signature maroon and green accents.
