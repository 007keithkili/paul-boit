News & Events

Stay updated with the latest happenings, achievements, and announcements from Paul Boit Boys.

FEATURED STORY · 15 MARCH 2026

Paul Boit Boys Crowned Regional Rugby Champions

Our under-19 rugby team brought home the trophy after a thrilling final against defending champions. The team's dedication and sportsmanship were on full display, with over 500 students cheering from the stands. This marks our third consecutive regional title. The victory qualifies the team for the national championships scheduled for later this year.

Recent News

10 MARCH 2026

Form 4 Candidates Excel in Mock Exams

Our Form 4 students achieved a mean score of 9.2 in the recent county-wide mocks, placing us second overall. Teachers attribute the success to extra revision sessions and individual mentoring. The results are a promising indicator for the upcoming KCSE exams.

28 FEBRUARY 2026

New Student Leadership Sworn In

Brian Kiprop was installed as the new Head Prefect in a colourful ceremony attended by parents and alumni. The 2026 cabinet pledged to enhance mentorship, improve school spirit, and maintain high standards of discipline throughout the year.

14 FEBRUARY 2026

Science Congress: 5 Projects Qualify for Nationals

Our young innovators impressed judges at the regional Science Congress with projects in robotics, environmental conservation, and renewable energy. Five teams will now represent the school at the national competition in Nairobi next month.

5 FEBRUARY 2026

Alumni Mentorship Week a Success

Old boys from various professions – including engineering, medicine, law, and business – returned to campus for a week of career talks and one-on-one mentoring. Students gained valuable insights into university choices and career paths.

22 JANUARY 2026

New Library Opens: A Modern Learning Hub

Thanks to donor contributions, our new library is now open. It features 5,000 books, e-learning stations, and quiet study areas. Students are already making full use of the facility, which also hosts weekly reading clubs.

10 JANUARY 2026

First Day of Term: Welcoming New Students

The 2026 academic year kicked off with 150 new Form 1 students. Orientation week includes team-building activities, campus tours, and introduction to school traditions. The new boys were warmly welcomed by the entire school community.
