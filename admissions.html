Welcome to Admissions

We are delighted that you are considering Paul Boit Boys High School for your son's secondary education. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, supportive, and focused on finding boys who will thrive in our community.

We welcome applicants from all backgrounds who share our values of integrity, resilience, and excellence. Whether joining us in Form 1 or transferring at a higher level, each boy is assessed individually to ensure a good fit.

Entry Requirements

Admission to Paul Boit Boys is competitive and based on the following criteria:

  • KCPE aggregate: 280 marks and above (for Form 1 entry)
  • Transfer students: KCSE mean grade of C+ or equivalent for Form 3 entry
  • Entrance interview: Conducted by the admissions panel
  • Character reference: From previous school headteacher
  • Birth certificate & passport photos: 4 copies

All applicants must demonstrate good conduct and a willingness to embrace our school values.

How to Apply

1

Inquiry

Contact our admissions office or fill the online inquiry form. We'll send you a prospectus and application pack.

2

Application

Submit completed application form, academic records, and supporting documents by the deadline.

3

Assessment

Shortlisted candidates attend an entrance interview and assessment day at the school.

4

Offer & Admission

Successful applicants receive an offer letter. Fees are paid and placement confirmed.

Fees & Financial Aid

We strive to keep our fees affordable while maintaining high standards. The annual tuition covers:

  • Full board (meals and accommodation)
  • Textbooks and learning materials
  • Sports and co-curricular activities
  • Basic medical care at the school clinic

Annual Fees (approximate)

KSh 85,000

Payable in three terms. Includes all levies.

Bursaries & Scholarships

We offer a limited number of need-based bursaries and merit scholarships for outstanding students. Contact our admissions office for details and application forms.
